About Us

SKYTEX for textiles was founded in the year 2011 by its founder, Engineer Mahmoud Ali. The Slogan of the company has always been "trust and honesty are the main reasons for success."
All the employees in this company have an astonishing experience and quality in printing, dyeing and processing.
Skytex is one of the major leading companies in the field of printing, dyeing and processing.
Skytex is one of the leading companies in the field of importing spare parts for printing, dyeing, processing and production requirements.

Skytex is also an exclusive agent for major Chinese, Italian and Turkish companies in the field of dyeing, printing and processing.
Due to the company's interest in all what is distinctive, the company has established a special department for maintenance at the highest level of engineers and technicians specialized in the maintenance of photocopiers and direct printing on canvas. The company has also won the trust of a large and broad base of customers of the textile industry in Egypt, because of the company's distinctive quality of products and outstanding prices and credibility in dealing and after-sales service.
